                                      Banasthali Vidyapith Prospectus
About Banasthali Vidyapith
Banasthali Vidyapith, established in 1935, is a premier university for women's education in India. Founded by eminent freedom fighters and educationists, the university is known for its commitment to holistic education and women empowerment.

Vision and Mission
Vision: To be a leading institution for women's education, research, and innovation, fostering global leaders with a sense of social responsibility.

Mission:

Provide quality education rooted in Indian values and ethos.
Empower women through education and skill development.
Promote interdisciplinary research and innovation.
Cultivate leadership qualities and ethical values.

Admission Procedure
Admission to Banasthali Vidyapith is based on merit and performance in the entrance examination conducted by the university. Eligibility criteria and admission process vary for each program. For detailed information, refer to the admission brochure or visit the university website.
