Course Title:Microelectronics 

Introduction to Microelectronics

Microelectronics is a field of electronics that deals with the design, fabrication, and application of tiny electronic components and circuits. These components typically range in size from a few micrometers to a few millimeters. Microelectronics has revolutionized various industries including telecommunications, computing, healthcare, and entertainment.
